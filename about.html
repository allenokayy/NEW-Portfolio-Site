about.

Heyo, thanks for checking out my website! My name is Allen Kay Jordan and I’m a recent graduate from Arizona State University. My degree had a focus on User Experience and had me perform user studies and redesigning websites based upon the feedback given, but that’s far from all I can do. I’m also knowledgeable about print production, brand design, social media advertising, and good ol’ Graphic Design in general.

To achieve great designs, I typically use Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, but I’m also familiar with Canva and Figma workflows and find them more useful for collaborative projects. I’m also very familiar with languages such as HTML and CSS when it comes to web projects. For a more complete list of my skills, feel free to reach out and ask for my resume!

I’m currently looking for Graphic Designer Internships to help me break into the industry and gain some more real world experience. If you like my work, I’d love to hear it and talk further. Thanks for your interest and I hope to hear from you soon!
